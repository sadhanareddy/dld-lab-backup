Introduction

    Welcome to the Digital Logic Design Lab. Here you can make basic combinational and sequential digital circuits of your choice and simulate them.

List of experiments
  • Adder Circuit
  • Multiplexer
  • Decoder with 7-Segment Display
  • ALU with function
  • Comparator
  • Latch and Flip Flops
  • Registers
  • Counters
  • Multipliers
  • State Digrams
Target Audience

    • Prior knowledge about basic logic gates is expected from a theory course
